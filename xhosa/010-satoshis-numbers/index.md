## USUKU LOKUZALWA LUKA-SATOSHI

* ***NgoAprili 5, 1975*** ngumhla uSatoshi awathi ungowakhe
usuku lokuzalwa.
* Nangona singenakukwazi ukuba lo ngumhla wakhe wokuzalwa wenene
umhla, inomdla kakhulu.
* ***NgoAprili 5th*** (1933) yayilusuku olathi uMyalelo olawulayo 6102
tyikitywe nguMongameli wase-US uFranklin D. Roosevelt
"ukwalela ukugcinwa kweengqekembe zegolide, igolide,
kunye nezatifikethi zegolide ngaphakathi kwilizwekazi
eUnited States.”
* ***1975*** yayingumnyaka wokurhoxiswa kwe-EO 6102 yangena ku
isebenze, kwaye abemi base-US baphinde bavunyelwa ukuba
bambe ngaphezulu kwe-5oz yegolide.

## IKHALENDA YEBITCOIN YEMIHLA EBALULEKILEYO

**2008-08-18** ~ Igama lesizinda **libhaliswe i-bitcoin.org.**

**2008-10-31** ~ **Usuku lwePhepha eliMhlophe leBitcoin:** IPhepha eliMhlophe,
elinomxholo othi "I-Bitcoin: Inkqubo yeMali ye-Electronic yoontanga-kuya-Ntoontanga" yayiyiyo
epapashwe ngumfihli-zinto ongaziwayo ogama linguSatoshi
Nakamoto kwi-metzdowd.com, uluhlu lokuthumela ngeposi lwe-cryptography.

**2009-01-03** ~ **Usuku lokuzalwa lweBitcoin:** Inethiwekhi yeBitcoin yayiyiyo
yasungulwa, xa uSatoshi wayimba i-Genesis block.

**2009-01-12** ~ **Utshintshiselwano lokuqala lwebitcoin** lwenzeka, xa uHal
UFenney ufumene ii-bitcoin ezilishumi kuSatoshi njengovavanyo lokuthumela.

**2009-10-05** ~ **Ukuzalwa kwendawo yokuqala yokutshintshiselana ngebitcoin,** I-New
I-Liberty Standard (NLS), enexabiso lentengiso elidwelisiweyo le-$0.00764
ngengqekembe nganye.

**2009-10-12** ~ "Ndifumene **i-bitcoin yokuqala eyaziwayo kutshintshiselwano lwe-USD** kwi-imeyile yam yogcino-lwazi. Ndithengise i-5,050 BTC nge-$5.02
ngomhla ka-2009-10-12." - Martti Malmi, umseki we-bitcointalk.org, wathengisa
i-bitcoin kwi-NewLibertyStandard eyaqala utshintshiselwano lokuqala.

**2010-05-22** ~ **Usuku lwePizza yeBitcoin:** Isiganeko sokuqala esaziwayo se

**2011-06-14** ~ **I-Wikileaks** iqala ukwamkela iminikelo nge-bitcoin.
